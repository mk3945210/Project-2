WELCOME TO COMPUTER REPAIR HUB

Computer repair technicians need a range of technical skills to excel in their profession. Troubleshooting is a crucial aspect, as is the ability to repair laptop and desktop computers. They should be adept at handling software issues, performing preventive maintenance, and installing computer software. Experience with various operating systems like Windows XP, Linux, and Mac is also beneficial. As Ming Chow, Associate Teaching Professor at Tufts University, notes, "Infrastructure, including cloud and networking, Security, Machine Learning, and Data Science" will become increasingly important for computer repair technicians in the next few years.In addition to these technical skills, soft skills are equally important for computer repair technicians. They should be able to communicate effectively with customers and colleagues, assisting them in using software and hardware. As Ming Chow also points out, "The soft skills, including communications, how to deal with change and the basics are becoming more critical-things like using a calendar, following-up, etc." Interpersonal skills are vital, as computer repair technicians often need to understand the needs of their customers and work with them to resolve issues.
